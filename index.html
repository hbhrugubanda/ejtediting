Home

EJT Editing offers structural editing, copy editing and proofreading services for any type of document. We are committed to polishing your work to the highest degree possible.

  • Structural editing – restructuring text for clear flow of ideas, clarity and readability; can include rewriting sections of text if necessary
  • Copy editing – editing for expression, house style, correct grammar, usage, spelling, punctuation, fact-checking and consistency of information
  • Proofreading – checking for correct grammar, usage, spelling and punctuation, as well as appropriate layout features such as page numbers, headers and spacing.
